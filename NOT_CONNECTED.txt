SECTION 1: ROUTING & PREDICTIONS (YOUR QUESTION)
================================================================================

PRIORITY RULE: ROUTING BEFORE AI
The system MUST calculate physical routing (road paths) before applying 
AI-based risk scores. We map the physical world first, then the smart layer.

Q: Is the "Live Prediction" and "Optimal Route" accurate?
A: [PARTIAL / HEURISTIC]

How it works right now:
1. DISTANCE: It uses real Euclidean distance (as the crow flies) between coordinates.
2. TIME: It uses the formula T = (D*K) + Φ + Ω found in Intelligence Hub.
3. ACCURACY: It is ACCURATE in terms of "Estimated Time" based on our math.
4. LIMITATION: It is NOT yet a turn-by-turn navigation engine. It does not 
   calculate the specific street-by-street path. It treats the distance as a 
   line and adds penalties for flood/traffic clusters that intersect that line.

[NOT CONNECTED] - Real Pathfinding: We do not yet have a C++ OSRM or GraphHopper 
backend to do full Dijkstra/A* routing on the KML road network.
