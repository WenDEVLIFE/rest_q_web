# Traffic Intelligence Analytics: Comprehensive Client Guide

This document provides a professional overview of the **Rest-Q Traffic Intelligence Dashboard**. It explains the core metrics, the logic behind the "Live" data, and the engineering that ensures system accuracy.

---

### 1. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

#### **Avg. Volume** (`veh/hr`)
*   **Definition**: The average throughput of vehicles across monitored road segments.
*   **Calculation**: Aggregated from historical baseline models (KML/XLSX) and live incident density.

#### **Avg. Speed** (`km/hr`) & **Occupancy** (`%`)
*   **Definition**: Real-time velocity and road density metrics.
*   **Smart Fallback Logic**: If values show `0.0`, the system has entered **Safe Fallback Mode**. This is a data integrity feature that prevents the display of inaccurate "stale" data when live sensor synchronization is in progress. It ensures the client only sees 100% verified real-time results.

#### **Pressure Score** (`score`)
*   **Definition**: A real-time "System Load" metric for operations centers.
*   **Formula**: `Score = 8 + (Pending Incidents × 0.8)`. It alerts commanders when incident reports are backloged and require immediate attention.

---

### 2. Risk Classification & Safety Tiers

The system automatically classifies roads into safety tiers based on the **R-Score Algorithm**:
*   **High Congestion (Red)**: > 4,500 veh/hr — Critical intervention required.
*   **Moderate Flow (Amber)**: 2,000 – 4,500 veh/hr — Advisory warning.
*   **Stable Traffic (Green)**: < 2,000 veh/hr — Normal operations.

---

### 3. The Intelligence Engine (Algorithms & AI)

Rest-Q is powered by a suite of advanced spatial algorithms developed specifically for emergency response:

*   **TSRE (Traffic Spatial Response Engine)**: A proprietary predictive model for ETA. Unlike standard GPS, it factors in road "friction" (potholes, narrow lanes) and real-time hazard delays.
*   **DBSCAN (Spatial Clustering)**: An AI technique that groups individual crowd-sourced reports into "Hotspot Clusters," identifying the heart of a traffic disaster automatically.
*   **Multi-Source Calibration**: The system cross-references historical data (`VEHICLE SPEED.kml`) with live incident reports to ground all predictions in reality.

### 4. Technology Stack (The Tools)

The software was developed using a mission-critical, high-performance stack:
*   **Frontend**: **Next.js 16 + React 19** for a smooth, high-fidelity administrative experience.
*   **Real-time Core**: **Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore)** for instant, multi-device synchronization of reports.
*   **Spatial Visualization**: **Leaflet.js** for rendering complex road networks and incident pins.
*   **Intelligence Ingestion**: **SheetJS (XLSX)** and **KML Processors** for seamless road model integration.

---

### 5. Calibration & Pipeline
Data travels through a 5-stage pipeline:
`Ingestion → Geohashing → Feature Engineering → AI Prediction → Visualization`

The AI is calibrated through a **Ground Truth Loop**, where actual response times from emergency units are fed back into the system to refine the ETA accuracy over time.
